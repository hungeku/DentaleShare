    About DentaleShare

    Learn about our team and culture.

     

    DentaleShare is an interoperable patient health records management system, empowering clinical care teams to share, manage and provide better health care to patients through an interactive platform without compromising the integrity and security of the data. Our goal is to develop and deliver the premier interoperable system where diverse healthcare systems and organizations can work seamlessly and securely to communicate and exchange electronic patient health information (ePHI) in real-time.

    We are committed to providing innovative solutions designed around the needs of clinical care teams and patients to deliver the best care possible. We believe we can make a difference by removing technological boundaries in healthcare and integrating innovative and affordable technology solutions with the entire patient entire care cycle. We develop and implement technology to improve our clients’ productivity and efficiency while ensuring data security and compliance with the HIPAA and HITECH rules and regulations.

    INNNOVATION
    We are constantly looking for ways to improve the quality of patient care, while maintaining a comprehensive approach to data security and integration.

    DIVERSITY
    We believe in the power of people connecting together under one platform to create better workflows and positive information exchange experiences.

    EXPERIENCE
    Our team has the experience and knowledge to understand the specifications and requirements of healthcare software and develop solutions to meet those needs. We offer a powerful culture of innovation, collaboration, and team effort to deliver the final results to our clients.

    ACCESSIBILITY
    Imagine a platform that allows multiple practices to benefit from instant communication and access to exchanged information without software limitations. We provide flexible access to electronic patient health information and easy exchange within your referral network.

    SECURITY
    We are committed to addressing the challenges of seamlessly and securely exchanging patient health information within the medical sector, and giving practices with different software systems the ability to easily exchange data and communicate.
